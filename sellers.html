Sellers

Lion Development Srl. acts as a sole buyer for property- and land sellers in the Sardinia region. We seek to provide you as a seller with the confidence, professionalism, speed, and level of service you may expect and deserve.

All purchases are made for (re-)Development and/ or exploitation are on our own book. Lion Development Srl. does not trade or act as a realtor.

When working with Lion Development, you can be confident that you are working with real estate professionals dedicated to the following principles:

  • Respect for Sardinia
  • Do what we say and say what we do
  • Superior service and communication
  • Total integrity and confidentiality
  • Sufficient financing
  • A drive for excellence

Please contact us if you have land or (commercial) real estate for sale. All inquiries are handled personally by a member of our team and always with the utmost integrity. We value personal contact.
